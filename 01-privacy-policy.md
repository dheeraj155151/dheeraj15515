# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** [INSERT DATE OF FIRST PUBLICATION]

This Privacy Policy describes how Arrow Mind ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile app.

## In short

We don't ask for your name, email, phone number, or contacts. We don't run accounts. We don't sell your data. We do show ads, and we do use analytics to understand how the app is used. Below is the detail.

## Information we collect

### Information you give us directly

None. The app does not ask for your name, email, phone number, or any personal account information.

### Information collected automatically

When you use Arrow Mind, the app and the third-party services it includes (listed below) may automatically receive certain technical information:

- **Device identifiers** — your Android Advertising ID (AAID) and an installation ID generated by Firebase. These are used to deliver ads and measure how the app is used.
- **Device information** — device model, Android OS version, language, country, mobile carrier, and screen size.
- **App interaction data** — which screens you open, which features you use, how long sessions last, and which puzzles you solve. This data is aggregated and used to improve the app.
- **Crash and performance data** — if the app crashes or hits an error, technical information about the crash is sent to us via Firebase Crashlytics.
- **Ad interaction data** — when ads are shown, watched, or clicked, AppLovin records this to bill advertisers and measure ad performance.

### Information we do NOT collect

- Your name, email, or phone number
- Your contacts
- Your location (we do not request location permission)
- Photos, files, microphone, camera, or any other sensitive device sensors
- Health, fitness, or biometric data

## Third-party services

The app uses these third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy.

### AppLovin MAX (advertising)

We use AppLovin MAX to show ads. AppLovin and its mediated ad partners may collect your Advertising ID, device information, and ad interaction data. AppLovin's privacy policy: https://www.applovin.com/privacy/

In the European Union, United Kingdom, and California (and other jurisdictions with similar laws), the app shows a consent dialog on first launch (the AppLovin UMP CMP) asking whether you consent to personalized ads. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalized to you.

### Google Firebase (analytics & crash reporting)

We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand how Arrow Mind is used and to detect crashes. Firebase may collect your Installation ID, app interaction events, and crash information. Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Google Play Services

Required for ads, analytics, and app distribution. Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Children's privacy

Arrow Mind is rated for ages 13 and up. It is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us at the email below and we will delete it.

## How long we keep your data

We do not store any personal data on our own servers. The data we use is held by the third-party services above, each according to its own retention policy. You can reset all on-device data at any time by uninstalling the app or by tapping Settings → Reset Progress in the app.

## Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

- Know what personal data is held about you
- Request a copy of that data
- Request correction or deletion of that data
- Withdraw your consent at any time (re-open the consent dialog via Settings → Privacy → Manage ad consent)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

For GDPR (EU/UK) and CCPA (California) specific requests, please specify your jurisdiction in your message.

## Children, ads, and "Designed for Families"

Arrow Mind contains ads and is targeted at users aged 13 and up. It is not enrolled in Google's "Designed for Families" program. We do not show personalized ads to users we believe to be under 13.

## Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will change the "Last updated" date at the top. Material changes will also be announced in the app's release notes.
